Whisperbot - Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 3, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains what data Whisperbot collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding your data.

2. Data We Collect

  • Server IDs – to identify the servers where the bot is active.
  • User IDs – for moderation actions, settings, and logs.
  • Message Content – only if required for moderation, logging, or custom commands (configurable).
  • Channel IDs & Names – to operate features like logging or auto-moderation in specific channels.

3. How We Use Your Data

Collected data is used solely to provide and improve the functionality of Whisperbot. Data is not sold or shared with third parties.

4. Data Storage

All data is stored securely and only accessible by the developers of Whisperbot. We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access or disclosure.

5. User Control

Server administrators can remove the bot at any time, which ceases all data collection. You may also request data deletion by contacting us directly.

6. Third-Party Services

Whisperbot may use third-party APIs (e.g., Discord API) as part of its functionality. We do not control how these services handle your data.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Major changes will be communicated via our support server.

8. Contact

If you have any privacy-related concerns, please contact us via Discord at @dualrapidmane or in our support server: https://discord.gg/low-life.
